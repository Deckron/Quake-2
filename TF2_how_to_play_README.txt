This mod is intended to bring certain team fortress 2 characters into quake 2
available characters are: scout, heavy, soldier, spy, sniper, and demo man
weapons have been changed to behave as if they were from team fortress 2
to gain a class, type into the console "give class"(demo for demo man, no caps)
spawning weapons via the console behaves normally
be careful with the scout class, it moves very fast
must be played with a mourse
